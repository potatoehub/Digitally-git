First we shall start with the most trending K-pop group internationaly. BTS,as we all guessed also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. They debuted on June 12, 2013 with the song "No More Dream" from their first album 2 Cool 4 Skool. The leader of the band is one of the main rappers with the name of Kim Nam-Joon (stage name Rm). The two others in the rap line are Min Yoon-gi (stage name Suga) and Jung Ho-seok (stage name J-Hope). Now lets get into the vocals; starting first in Kim Seok-jin (stage name Jin) also the eldest member in the band, next is Park Jimin (stage name Jimin) then there is always Kim Tae-hyung (stage name V) last but not least the youngest memeber is Jeon Jungkook (stage name Jungkook)!
